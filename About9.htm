FleetPro


RentPro™ Customers benefit from faster turnaround time in customer set-up, vehicle ordering, and customer service because there are specific RentPro teams designated to assist in these functions - FLEET PRO.

RentPro™ Fleet management module takes over day-to-day management and cost control of your vehicle maintenance & administration

RentPro™ Fleet management program for vehicles can:

  • Provide a comprehensive analysis of company's needs and existing fleet, resulting in better equipment at less cost.
  • Reduce repetitive administrative tasks associated with day-to-day operations, allowing fleet managers more time to concentrate on their core competency
  • Facilitate compliance to preventive maintenance schedules, which will mean fewer & less costly corrective repairs.
  • Minimize unscheduled repairs to reduce vehicle downtime and car rental costs.
  • Increase productivity and profitability.
  • Provide complete maintenance tracking reports.
  • A fuel card system.
  • A maintenance and repair program.
  • A complete inventory for vehicles accessories and tyres.
